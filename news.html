Award Winning Peer Mentor Scheme

Dr Eve Binks & Dr Catherine Thompson

The School of Psychology is thrilled to announce that Eve and Catherine are the worthy winners of the Liverpool Hope Learning and Teaching Prize for 2024, announced at Foundation Day.

Eve & Catherine worked in partnership with students from Level I and Level H to develop & support a Psychology Peer Mentor Scheme.

If you want to access support from the peer mentors you can go to the mentors page to find out more.

BPS and ATSiP Technical Support in
Psychological Teaching Award

Dr Glen Pennington

Each year the British Psychology Society accepts nominations for the Technical Support in Psychological Teaching Award. The Teaching Award recognises the key role that technicians play in supporting the learning experience of students in psychology departments. This year, the Teaching Award was presented to Glen Pennington from Liverpool Hope University.

Student Success - Marie Stamp

Psychology Masters student (Applied Developmental Psychology) Marie Stamp's Undergraduate dissertation was selected for an award by the BPS (she received a prize of £100 Amazon vouchers) and the chance to appear in their publication. After submitting her dissertation for review, she had to engage in a process to summarise the research quite considerably for the publication.

To read Marie's work visit the BPS Webpage.
